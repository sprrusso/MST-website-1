The Facility

The UCMST is housed in the Joan C. Sergent Instructional Resource Center, a specially designed facility that includes the highest level of current technology. State of the art instructional components are featured in every science laboratory, computer laboratory, and classroom.

Utica Community Schools

It is the second largest district in the state of Michigan and serves over 29,000 students. Located fifteen miles north of Detroit, it is characteristically suburban, encompassing two municipalities and four townships. The community is largely residential with strong economic ties to the automotive industry. The UCS has enjoyed a long-standing tradition of exceptional citizen support, which has resulted in numerous innovative educational programs such as the UCMST.
